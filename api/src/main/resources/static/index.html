Today’s social media exploits your data, makes you addicted and spreads hate.

Ideell wants be the new social media platform. It’ll protect users’ privacy and implement humanist principles.

Ideell will be a Swedish ideell förening and seeks future members and donations.

Ideell's Manifest

  1. The services are provided by an independent non-profit non-governmental organization or foundation
  2. Never sells or exposes users’ data but user may download their own data
  3. Strong humanistic principles that both the organization and users are expected to follow
    1. Pro Democracy
    2. Pro Science
    3. Pro Human Rights
    4. Responsible Publisher
  4. Rely on human moderation
  5. Rely on authenticated users. But user may hide their identity from the public
  6. Financed by
    1. Donations
    2. Sponsors
    3. No commercial advertisement
  7. No use of tracking technologies like cookies
  8. Use open source as much as possible
